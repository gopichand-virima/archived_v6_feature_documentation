# Edit or Delete Scheduled Scan

Use this function to manage a scheduled scan.


The **Related Scans** information is view only. It displays all previously triggered scans.


## Edit Scheduled Scan

In the Scheduled Scans window, select a record to edit.

Modify any of the following information:

- [Details](/discovery/scans_scheduled_new_6_1.md)
- [Tasks](/discovery/ad_user_import_tasks_6_1.md)
- [Comments](/discovery/ad_user_import_comments_6_1.md)
- [Attachments](/discovery/ad_user_import_attachments_6_1.md)

When all selections/entries are made, click **Save**.
